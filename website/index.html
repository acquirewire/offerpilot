    07 / FAQ

    Straight answers.

    Will it keep my CV's exact formatting?

    Yes. It edits your Word document in place — only the words change. Fonts, spacing, layout and structure stay exactly as you built them.

    Is the one page really guaranteed?

    Yes — and not by guessing. It renders the actual PDF, counts the real pages, and intelligently condenses the least job-relevant detail until it physically fits a single page.

    Will it make things up?

    Never. It re-angles and re-words what you actually did, keeps every real number, and leaves your grades and facts untouched. No invented experience, full stop.

    Does it only work for finance?

    No — it reads the requirements from whatever job description you paste, so consulting, tech, law and graduate schemes work just as well.

    Is my CV private?

    Your documents are processed only to generate your tailored CV and are never sold or shared. You control every file.
