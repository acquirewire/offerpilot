FAQ

Questions, answered

Does it keep my CV’s formatting?

Yes — it edits your existing Word document in place, changing only the words. Fonts, columns, spacing and layout stay exactly as you designed them.

Is it really always one page?

Guaranteed. It renders the real PDF, checks the page count, and intelligently condenses the least-relevant detail until it fits — never overflowing, never leaving the page half-empty.

Will it invent experience I don’t have?

No. It only re-frames and re-words your real achievements to match the role, and never fabricates. Your grades and facts are left untouched.

Which jobs does it work for?

Any role with a job description. It reads the requirements from each posting, so it’s just as sharp for consulting, tech or graduate schemes as it is for finance.

Is my data private?

Your CV is processed only to generate your documents and is never sold or shared. You stay in control of every file.
