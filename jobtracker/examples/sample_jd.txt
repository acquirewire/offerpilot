2027 Summer Analyst — Investment Banking Division (Hong Kong)

About the role
Our Investment Banking Division advises corporations, institutions and governments
on mergers and acquisitions, and on raising capital in the equity and debt markets.
As a Summer Analyst you will support live deal teams across the full transaction
lifecycle.

Responsibilities
- Build and maintain financial models, including DCF, LBO and comparable company
  and precedent transaction analyses
- Prepare client presentations and pitch books in PowerPoint
- Conduct industry and company due diligence and research

Requirements
- Penultimate-year student graduating in 2027
- Strong analytical skills and proficiency in Excel and financial modeling
- Excellent attention to detail and ability to work under pressure
- Fluency in English required; Cantonese or Mandarin strongly preferred for the
  Hong Kong office
- Demonstrated interest in finance and capital markets
